# What should I build?

A few interesting (suggested) directions you could build in:

1. **Trust, Commerce & Fraud...** Build systems that make online transactions safer, reduce scams, detect fraud, and increase trust in digital marketplaces.

2. **The Future of Work...** Help people navigate an increasingly volatile job market and access better career opportunities.

3. **Security, Resilience & Defense...** Strengthen national, organizational, and individual resilience against physical and cyber threats.

4. **AI-Native Organizations...** Help businesses transition from traditional workflows to AI-powered, agent-driven operations. Rapidly. At scale.

These are suggestions, not constraints. The best projects often come from a problem you've personally experienced and deeply understand.

Build2026 is a Proof of Work hackathon. Our VC, ecosystem, and startup partners will be on the ground to scout top talent & ideas.
