# Presentation Outline: Zo Relationship Mapper

10 slides max. Goal: communicate the opportunity, wedge, product, demo, and business potential without turning the deck into feature documentation.

## Slide 1: Title

**Zo Relationship Mapper**

Turn career history and proof-of-work into warm trust paths for better opportunities.

Message:

- Cold applications are low-trust.
- Warm paths are career leverage.

## Slide 2: Why Now

AI has made applications, resumes, and outreach cheap to generate.

Message:

- Content is abundant.
- Trust is scarce.
- The next career edge is knowing which relationship path to activate.

## Slide 3: User

Mid-career and senior working professionals with 5-20 years of experience.

Beachhead:

- 7-15 years experience.
- Software, AI, product, consulting, finance, sustainability, operations.
- Already have network density and willingness to pay for career leverage.

## Slide 4: Problem

People have useful weak ties, alumni links, ex-colleagues, communities, and proof-of-work, but they do not know which path is strongest for a specific opportunity.

Current workarounds:

- LinkedIn search.
- Manual memory.
- Notes and spreadsheets.
- Personal CRM tools.
- Cold applications.

Core gap:

- These track contacts, not opportunity-specific trust paths.

## Slide 5: Product

Paste profile. Enter target. Get ranked trust paths and a contextual next action.

Flow:

1. Parse career profile.
2. Discover relevant public people/company signals.
3. Rank trust paths.
4. Explain why each path is credible.
5. Draft warm outreach.
6. Track outcome.

## Slide 6: Demo

Demo narrative:

> Mid-career AI/software professional targets an AI product/platform role at a climate tech startup in Singapore.

Show:

- Profile loaded.
- Target entered.
- Exa-style people discovery.
- Ranked trust paths.
- Outreach draft.
- Outcome marked as contacted.

Demo tooling:

- HyperFrames for external demo video.
- Driver.js for in-app guided workflow.

## Slide 7: Differentiation

Not a CRM. Not a career coach. Not a job board.

Sharp wedge:

- Target-first, not contact-first.
- Career-context-aware.
- Proof-of-work-aware.
- Trust-path ranking, not generic networking advice.
- Next-action oriented.

## Slide 8: Business Model

Start with a paid sprint, not broad SaaS.

Initial monetization:

- 30-day Trust Path Sprint.
- Pay when actively exploring a role, company, founder path, consulting opportunity, or career move.

Possible pricing:

- Individual sprint: S$49-S$149.
- Premium career/operator segment: S$199+ for higher-stakes moves.

## Slide 9: Expansion

Start with career access, then expand into broader opportunity access.

Expansion paths:

- Founder fundraising warm paths.
- Freelancer and consultant client access.
- Advisor/operator opportunity mapping.
- Community and alumni network intelligence.
- Proof-of-work based reputation graph.

## Slide 10: Ask / Next Milestones

What needs validation:

- Will mid-career professionals trust the ranking?
- Will they send the suggested outreach?
- Do they get replies, intros, referrals, or advice calls?
- Will they pay for a Trust Path Sprint?

Next milestones:

- Test with 20 target users.
- Measure outreach sent and reply rate.
- Improve trust scoring explanations.
- Add import paths only after manual workflow proves demand.
