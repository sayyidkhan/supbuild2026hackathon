## the idea i have
Building Zo Expert: a digital twin / consultation proxy template for small business owners.

## the problem statement
Many business owners realise their time is limited.
They are bounded to 24 hours, but customers, prospects, and staff still want access to their judgment, standards, and expertise.
How can they scale their time while still conforming to their standards?

## proposed solution
Build a digital twin template on Zo Computer. Business owners can scale their time, effort, and results because they now have a digital consultant that acts as a proxy for people asking business questions.

The first version should be simple: non-technical users ask questions, Zo Expert answers from the owner's business knowledge and tone, then escalates what needs the actual owner.

The business can be scaled further using Zo's managed service and optional agentic services. Everything can be accessed through the portal and managed in-house or on-prem within the Zo Computer ecosystem.

the idea:
- scale the owner's judgment
- reduce repeated questions
- help customers and staff get owner-style guidance
- escalate sensitive or unusual questions safely
- preserve the owner's standards while increasing access
