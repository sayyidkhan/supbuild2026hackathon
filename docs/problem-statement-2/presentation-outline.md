# Presentation Outline: Zo Expert

10 slides max. Goal: communicate the owner bottleneck, practical wedge, product workflow, demo, and monetization path without making it sound like a generic chatbot.

## Slide 1: Title

**Zo Expert**

An AI consultation proxy for SME owners.

Message:

- Small businesses do not need abstract AI transformation.
- They need scalable access to the owner's judgment.

## Slide 2: Why Now

AI can now answer in context, but most SME knowledge still lives inside the owner.

Message:

- Owners are the router, memory, salesperson, support lead, and decision engine.
- Repeated questions and small decisions do not scale.
- The opportunity is turning owner judgment into a safe consultation layer.

## Slide 3: Buyer And User

Buyer:

- SME owner-operator.
- S$100k-S$750k annual revenue.
- 1-8 staff.
- Appointment-led, quote-led, or expertise-led services.

End user:

- Customers.
- Prospects.
- Staff.

## Slide 4: Problem

The owner is the operating bottleneck.

Examples:

- Customers ask repeated questions.
- Prospects wait for guidance before booking.
- Staff ask for exceptions and decisions.
- Slow replies leak revenue.
- Delegation is hard because business logic is in the owner's head.

## Slide 5: Product

Owner defines business knowledge, tone, policies, FAQs, and escalation rules.

Then customers or staff ask questions through a simple consultation portal.

Flow:

1. Owner loads or builds a template.
2. App captures services, rules, tone, and escalation boundaries.
3. User asks a question.
4. AI answers in owner style when safe.
5. Risky questions escalate.
6. Owner gets a brief of consultations and gaps.

## Slide 6: Demo

Demo narrative:

> A small appointment-led service business uses Zo Expert to answer repeated customer questions while escalating exceptions.

Show:

- Admin workspace.
- Sample owner template.
- User portal.
- Common customer question.
- Owner-style answer.
- Risky exception question.
- Escalation and owner brief.

Demo tooling:

- HyperFrames for external demo video.
- Driver.js for in-app guided workflow.

## Slide 7: Differentiation

Not a generic chatbot. Not a CRM. Not enterprise automation.

Sharp wedge:

- Owner-context-first.
- Business-memory-first.
- Escalation-aware.
- Built for non-technical users.
- Focused on consultation, not autonomous execution.

## Slide 8: Business Model

Start with a practical SME subscription.

Initial monetization:

- Setup fee for owner template creation.
- Monthly subscription for consultation portal and owner brief.

Possible pricing:

- Setup: S$199-S$499.
- Monthly: S$49-S$199 depending on usage and vertical.

## Slide 9: Expansion

Start with repeated consultation questions, then expand into owner operating leverage.

Expansion paths:

- Staff expert portal.
- Customer pre-sales guidance.
- Knowledge gap updates.
- Booking and payment handoff.
- WhatsApp or website widget later.
- Vertical templates for clinics, tuition, beauty, renovation, home services, and agencies.

## Slide 10: Ask / Next Milestones

What needs validation:

- Will owners trust it to answer repeated questions?
- Which questions do they want answered vs escalated?
- Does it save time or improve conversion?
- Which vertical has the clearest willingness to pay?

Next milestones:

- Interview 10 SME owners.
- Test with one vertical first.
- Measure repeated-question volume.
- Validate setup willingness and monthly pricing.
- Add integrations only after the consultation workflow proves demand.
